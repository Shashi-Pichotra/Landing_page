Guru Jambheshwar University, Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology (GJUST) is a state level university which is located in Hisar, Haryana. It was established in 1995 by an act of the legislature of the State of Haryana. The University is approved by the UGC and the AICTE. It is a member of the AIU and is accredited by the NAAC Grade ‘A’. GJUST offers several UG, PG, PG diploma, diploma, and PhD programmes under 10 faculties
